MyCityFix isn't just an app; it's a movement. We are a team of passionate citizens leveraging technology to empower our community and build a more responsive, beautiful city.

How MyCityFix Works

1. Pin Issues on a Map

Easily mark the exact location of civic problems directly on an interactive map. Our geo-tagging ensures precise reporting.

2. Upload Visual Proof

Attach photos or short videos to your reports. Visual evidence makes issues clearer and helps authorities understand the scope.

3. Track Progress & Resolution

Monitor the status of reported issues in real-time, from "Pending" to "In Progress" and ultimately "Resolved" by local officials.
